By default, dunst is autostarted by DBus. This can be turned off by editing `/usr/share/dbus-1/services/org.knopwob.dunst.service` and changing

```desktop
Exec=/usr/bin/dunst
```

To:

```desktop
Exec=/usr/bin/true
```
